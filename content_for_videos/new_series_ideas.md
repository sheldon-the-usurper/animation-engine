---

**Option 4 — The Science of Impossibility**
A series on things mathematically proven to be forever impossible — not hard, not unsolved, but *provably unreachable*. Each episode ends on a deeper impossibility than the last.

---

**Option 5 — The Biggest Ideas Nobody Teaches**
Five foundational concepts that quietly run the modern world — information theory, entropy, emergence, feedback loops, and phase transitions — told as one connected story nobody has connected before.
